by [George Orwell](https://www.goodreads.com/author/show/3706.George_Orwell)

If you hope to read a novel with a happy ending, this is not a book for you. Nineteen Eighty-Four is a work about the consequences of government over-reach,
mass surveillance, totalitarism and repressive regimentation. A work of art that still inspires thoughts even if indirectly, you must have already heard someone
say at least one of those words in your life: Big Brother, Doublethink, Thoughtcrime, Newspeak, Room 101, Telescreen, 2 + 2 = 5, Prole, and Memory Hole. For me, this book is a must-read for anyone who wants to start understanding politics.
